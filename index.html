About

Photography rooted
in the natural world

Hi, I'm Fraser. I am a landscape photographer and graduate student with a deep love for the natural world and beautiful light.

I've had a camera in my hand since I was 12. My dad's an avid photographer; his grandfather was a professional photographer in the 1950s in the highlands of Scotland. Without running water or electricity, my great-grandfather processed his film in a small brook in Rothiemurchus, near Aviemore, Scotland.

Alongside my landscape work, I photograph people during moments that matter: weddings, graduations, families, and personal milestones.

Based in New Haven, CT, and available for bookings throughout the NE.
